About Me

Hello! I am a class of 2016 graduate from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I received a B.S. in Advertising. After college, I worked as an account person at two advertising agencies. During my time there, I found that I was interested in the more technical project like building a website for a client or working on Customer Relations Manager (CRM) systems or creating an excel program that easily . I decided that instead of being the middle man between the client and development team, I wanted to be a part of the development team and build these technologies. So I started learning how to code at Coding Temple which leads me here.

Why Coding?

I touched on my desire to learn how to code earlier, but my interest started back in college. As a part of my curriculum, I had to take an Intro to Computer Science class. The class ended up being one of my favorite courses that I took while in college, and I felt a strong sense of accomplishment when my code worked. The following summer, I used the knowledge learned in that class to build a program for the bank I was interning at that I am told was still used long after my internship ended. As I learned more during my time at Coding Temple, I have found a new appreciation for the power of code and all of its uses.
